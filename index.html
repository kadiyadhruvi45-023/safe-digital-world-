About Safe Digital World

Safe Digital World is a Cyber Security Awareness platform. Our mission is to educate everyone about online safety, digital privacy, cyber attacks, password security, and cyber crime prevention.

Common Cyber Threats

Hacking

Hackers can steal your personal information.

Phishing

Fake emails and fake websites steal passwords.

Malware

Malicious software damages your computer.

Password Attack

Weak passwords are easy to crack. Always use strong passwords.

Network Attack

Public Wi-Fi can expose your personal data.

Bank Fraud

Never share your OTP or banking details with anyone.

Cyber Safety Tips

Strong Password

Use at least 12 characters with numbers, symbols and letters.

Don't Open Unknown Emails

Avoid clicking unknown links and attachments.

Enable 2FA

Turn on Two-Factor Authentication on every important account.
